Worcester Source is a monthly meetup group hosted on the fourth Wednesday of each month in the back-room of the Paul Pry pub in central Worcester.

Each meet features time for networking and refreshments, the featured presentation, and a round table discussion which extends the themes of the talk, with a “what I learnt” discussion the following day on the group’s Slack channel.

Vision

To inspire, motivate, and connect technical professionals in Worcestershire.

Mission

To build a community of technical professionals by hosting regularly-scheduled talks and seminars on topics of interest presented by experts in their field.

What is a Technical Professional?

We believe that most people who fit into this group are self-identifying, but for everyone else, this group includes but isn't limited to:

  • Developers and programmers
  • System administrators
  • Technical business owners
  • Product and project managers
  • Designers
  • Social media and marketing specialists
